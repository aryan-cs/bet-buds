2 Formats:
    1. Randomized Bingo Board
        Each group member gets a randomly placed bingo board with a bunch of bets that everyone inputted.
        Whoever gets bingo first wins.
    2. Parlay
        At the beginning, players all rate bets on a scale of 1 to 5 stars, 5 being the most difficult.
        Group members pick a number of bets to make a parlay. 
        Each bet in the parlay has a point value attached to it based on the ratings.
        Whoever gets the most points wins.
